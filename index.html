About Us

Small Studio, Real Games

FunMindo Studio is an independent mobile game studio based in Maharashtra, India. We build games that respect players' time — no forced ads, no predatory monetisation, just games designed to be genuinely fun.

Every game we ship is free to download and play in full, supported only by optional advertising. We believe good games don't need to trick you into spending money to be worth playing.
