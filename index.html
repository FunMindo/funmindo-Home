Stack It!

A precision stacking game — drop blocks with a single tap, trim every overhang, and climb as high as you can. Beat your personal best and compete on global leaderboards.

How to Play
1
Watch the Block

A block slides above your tower. Focus on its position and timing.

2
Tap to Drop

Perfect alignment keeps the block full size. Off-centre = trimmed.

3
Keep Stacking

Blocks get faster and smaller as you climb. Stay focused.

4
Climb the Leaderboard

Submit your score to weekly and all-time global rankings.

Why You'll Love It
🏆
Global Leaderboards

Weekly and all-time rankings. Pick a display name — no personal info needed.

🆓
Completely Free

No pay-to-win. Supported by optional ads — free forever.

👨‍👩‍👧
Safe for All Ages

Rated Everyone 3+. Pre-curated leaderboard names — no inappropriate entries.

🔒
No Account Required

Play anonymously. Progress lives on your device. No sign-up, ever.

Brain Surge

A fast, offline trivia arcade. Jump into Lightning rounds, go head-to-head in Duels, or keep a Daily Challenge streak alive — all without needing a connection.

How to Play
1
Pick a Mode

Lightning for speed, Endless for stamina, Duel to go head-to-head, or the daily Question of the Day.

2
Answer Fast

Every question is timed — accuracy and speed both add to your score.

3
Build a Streak

Come back daily for the Daily Challenge and keep your streak going.

4
Unlock & Customize

Earn XP as you play and unlock new visual themes for your board.

Why You'll Love It
📴
Play Offline

No internet required — quiz anywhere, anytime.

⚡
Multiple Modes

Lightning, Endless, Duel, and a daily challenge — pick your pace.

🔥
Daily Streaks

Build a habit with a fresh Question of the Day, every day.

Free Forever
